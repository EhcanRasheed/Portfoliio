Introduction

Born in 2002 in Gujarkhan with a knack for curiosity and learning. I scored 88% in Matric and was always eager to understand everything around me. I had a fascination with many things and subjects, and technology was one of them, especially computers, inspired by a cousin who was a Software Engineer. Although I didn’t fully grasp what software engineering was at the time, my interest was sparked. My aspirations then shifted towards the Military, aiming to become an Army Officer, but life had different plans. I redirected my focus to Computer Science, gaining admission to Capital University Islamabad for a BS in CS. Currently, I’m delving into Web Development and just started exploring the .NET Framework. Looking ahead, I aspire to venture into the Cyber Security industry.
